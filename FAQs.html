How do Create my tasks?

Simply go to Create task and fill in the required details and thats all!

How do I view tasks that are claimable?

Open up the Task Stream page and you will be presented with all the unclaimed tasks on the website!

How do I edit my personal information?

Easy! just open Edit details and you will be presented with a form similar to registration

How do I Claim a task?

In view details you will be presented with a button to claim that task.

If I see a task I feel is innappropriate what should I do?

In view details you will be presented with a button to Flag that task.

Can I download a preview of a task

In view details you will be presented with a button to download a preview for that task.

How can I achieve moderator status?

Earn a reputation score greater that 40 and you will be set
